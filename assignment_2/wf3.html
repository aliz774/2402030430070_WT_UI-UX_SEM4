Rome, known as the "Eternal City", is a historical and cultural hub brimming with ancient ruins, baroque squares, and world-renowned art, making it a must-visit destination. Some of the most popular tourist attractions include the Colosseum, Roman Forum, Pantheon, Trevi Fountain, and Vatican City. The city's rich history spans over two millennia, offering visitors a unique blend of classical antiquity and modern vibrancy. From the bustling Piazza Navona to the serene gardens of Villa Borghese, there's always something new to discover in this timeless metropolis.
